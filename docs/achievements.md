# Achievements
Dank RPG currently has 4 achievements, each giving rewards. These are:

## 1. On another Level:
Achieved by upgrading to 2ATK.
Rewards: 150 Coins

## 2. Your First Time:
Achieved by claiming your daily reward for the first time (`d!daily`).
Rewards: 100 Coins

## 3. Slots Addict:
Achieved by spending a total of 1000 Coins on `d!slots`. You can check your total spent on slots with `d!achievements`.
Rewards: 100 Coins

## 4. The Family Farm:
Achieved by running the `d!farm` command for the first time.
Rewards: 50 Coins
