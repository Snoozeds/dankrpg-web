Explains all your skills.

```
MaxHP
The maximum amount of HP you can have.

HP
Your current HP.

Armor
Reduces your damage taken (armor * 100%).

Damage
Increases your chance of winning fights.

Level
You gain more rewards per level. You gain XP by using commands.
Each level, you get +5 damage (max 25), +100hp, +100maxHP, +1 armor.```
